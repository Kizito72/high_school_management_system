January 15, 2026
Annual Science Fair Winners Announced

Our students showcased exceptional innovation at this year's science fair. First place went to Maria Borg and Thomas Vella for their project on renewable energy solutions for Malta. The fair featured 45 projects across various scientific disciplines.

The winning project demonstrated a novel approach to harnessing solar energy using locally-available materials, attracting interest from industry professionals.

January 10, 2026
New STEM Laboratory Opening Ceremony

We are thrilled to announce the opening of our new state-of-the-art STEM laboratory, funded through a partnership with the Malta Ministry of Education and local technology companies.

The facility includes robotics equipment, 3D printers, and advanced computing systems to enhance our science and technology programs.

January 5, 2026
Students Excel in National Mathematics Competition

Three of our students placed in the top 10 nationally in the Malta Mathematics Olympiad. Sarah Camilleri secured 3rd place, while Luke Azzopardi and Emma Farrugia placed 7th and 9th respectively.

This achievement reflects the strength of our mathematics program and our students' dedication to academic excellence.

December 20, 2025
Winter Concert Spectacular Success

Our annual winter concert featured performances by the school orchestra, choir, and various student ensembles. Over 300 parents and community members attended the sold-out event.

Special recognition to Music Director Mr. Anthony Grech and all student performers for their outstanding presentations.

December 15, 2025
Basketball Team Wins Regional Championship

Our under-16 basketball team clinched the regional championship title with a thrilling 78-72 victory in the final match. Team captain Daniel Zammit was named Most Valuable Player of the tournament.

The team will represent our region in the national finals scheduled for March 2026.

December 10, 2025
Environmental Club Beach Cleanup Initiative

Members of our Environmental Club organized a successful beach cleanup at Golden Bay, collecting over 200kg of litter. The initiative was part of our ongoing commitment to environmental sustainability.

The club meets every Tuesday and welcomes new members interested in environmental conservation.
