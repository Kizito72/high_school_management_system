Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1985

Solid State High School was founded in 1985 with a vision to provide world-class education to the youth of Malta. Located in the vibrant town of Birkirkara, our institution has grown from a modest beginning with just 120 students to become one of Malta's most prestigious secondary schools, serving over 850 students today.

Our founders, Dr. Anthony Farrugia and Mrs. Elena Vella, believed that education should prepare students not just for examinations, but for life itself. This philosophy continues to guide us today, as we combine rigorous academic standards with character development, critical thinking, and real-world skills.

Over the past four decades, we have produced thousands of successful graduates who have gone on to excel in universities and careers around the world, bringing honor to Malta and making meaningful contributions to society.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, student-centered education that develops intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and strong moral character. We strive to create an inclusive learning environment where every student can discover their potential and achieve excellence.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Malta's leading educational institution, known for academic excellence, innovative teaching, and producing well-rounded graduates who are prepared to lead and serve in an increasingly complex and interconnected world.

Our Core Values

The Principles That Guide Us

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in everything we do, encouraging students to reach their full potential academically, socially, and personally.

Integrity

We foster honesty, responsibility, and ethical behavior in all aspects of school life, preparing students to be principled citizens.

Respect

We celebrate diversity and promote mutual respect, creating an inclusive community where everyone feels valued and supported.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas and technologies, continuously improving our teaching methods to provide cutting-edge education.

Community

We build strong partnerships with families and the wider community, recognizing that education is a shared responsibility.

Global Citizenship

We prepare students to thrive in a globalized world, fostering cultural awareness and environmental responsibility.
