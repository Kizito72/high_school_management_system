Our Curriculum

Comprehensive Education for Grades 7-12

Sciences

Biology, Chemistry, Physics

Our science programs emphasize hands-on learning with modern laboratories. Students engage in experiments, research projects, and scientific inquiry from foundational concepts to advanced topics.

  • State-of-the-art laboratory equipment
  • Annual science fair participation
  • Environmental studies program
  • Partnership with University of Malta

Mathematics

Pure Math, Applied Math, Statistics

Progressive mathematics education from algebra and geometry through calculus and statistics. We develop problem-solving skills and logical thinking essential for all fields.

  • Differentiated instruction levels
  • Mathematics competition preparation
  • Computer-aided learning tools
  • Advanced placement options

Languages & Literature

Maltese, English, Italian, French

Multilingual education preparing students for global citizenship. Strong emphasis on literature, communication skills, and cultural understanding.

  • Native and foreign language programs
  • Literature and creative writing
  • International exchange programs
  • Language certification preparation

Humanities & Social Sciences

History, Geography, Social Studies

Critical examination of human societies, cultures, and historical events. Students develop analytical skills and global perspective.

  • Maltese and European history
  • Current affairs discussions
  • Field trips and cultural visits
  • Model UN participation

Technology & Computer Science

Programming, Web Design, Robotics

Comprehensive technology education preparing students for the digital age. From basic computer literacy to advanced programming.

  • Python, Java, and web development
  • Robotics and AI fundamentals
  • 3D printing and CAD design
  • Cybersecurity awareness

Arts & Creative Studies

Visual Arts, Music, Drama

Fostering creativity and artistic expression through various mediums. Students explore and develop their artistic talents.

  • Art studio and digital design
  • Music theory and performance
  • Drama and theater productions
  • Annual art exhibitions

Enrichment & Support

Beyond the Classroom

Learning Support

Individualized support for students with diverse learning needs, including tutoring and special education services.

Gifted Program

Advanced enrichment opportunities for high-achieving students including accelerated courses and research projects.

Clubs & Activities

Over 30 student clubs including debate, chess, coding, environmental, and cultural clubs.

About Solid State High School

Excellence in education since 1985. We are committed to nurturing young minds and preparing them for a successful future.
