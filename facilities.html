State-of-the-Art Campus

Modern Facilities Supporting Excellence in Education

Science Laboratories

Three fully-equipped laboratories for Biology, Chemistry, and Physics with modern equipment and safety features. Each lab accommodates 30 students and includes digital microscopes, fume hoods, and interactive displays.

Computer Laboratories

Two state-of-the-art computer labs with 40 workstations each, equipped with latest software for programming, design, and digital learning. High-speed internet connectivity throughout campus.

Learning Resource Centre

Extensive library with over 15,000 books, digital resources, and quiet study areas. Includes a research section, multimedia room, and comfortable reading spaces for independent learning.

Sports Complex

Indoor gymnasium, outdoor football pitch, basketball courts, and fitness centre. Facilities support our comprehensive physical education program and after-school sports activities.

Auditorium & Performance Hall

450-seat auditorium with professional lighting and sound systems for assemblies, performances, and presentations. Adjacent drama studio for rehearsals and classes.

Cafeteria & Dining Hall

Modern cafeteria serving nutritious meals and snacks. Seating for 200 students with indoor and outdoor dining areas. Menu designed by nutritionists to support student health.
