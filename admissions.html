Join Our School Community

We welcome applications from motivated students who are eager to learn and grow. Our admissions process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our challenging and supportive environment.

Application Period

October 1 - January 31

Applications open in October for the following academic year starting in September.

Grade Levels

Forms 1-5 (Ages 11-16)

We accept students for all secondary school levels based on availability.

Entrance Exam

March

Assessment in Mathematics, English, and Reasoning Skills.

Admission Requirements

Completed Application Form

Submit the online application form with all required information.

Academic Records

Previous two years' report cards and transcripts from current school.

Birth Certificate

Certified copy of student's birth certificate.

Recommendation Letters

Two recommendation letters from current teachers or school administrators.

Entrance Assessment

Successful completion of our entrance examination and interview.

Application Process

Submit Application

Complete and submit the online application form along with required documents.

Document Review

Our admissions team reviews all submitted materials.

Entrance Exam

Eligible students are invited to take our entrance assessment.

Interview

Qualified candidates attend an interview with staff members.

Decision

Admission decisions are communicated by April 15th.

Enrollment

Accepted students complete enrollment and attend orientation.
